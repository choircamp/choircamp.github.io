WHAT

Use this page to print registration and medical forms for camp (see below). After you print them, these forms must be submitted via postal mail. They may not be faxed, emailed, or submitted via this website.

There are two ways to send in your payment: 1) include it with your postal mail or 2) make a payment here using Paypal (see below).

If you have any questions or feedback, please see the Contact Information on our homepage.

Registration is finalized only after full payment is received. The cost of the registration is:

If you submit payment via postal mail, include the payment when you mail in your registration forms (see above for address).

When paying online, submit a single payment, even if you're registering multiple campers. Type the name of the camper(s) whose registration you're purchasing. This helps ensure your payment is applied to the right registration. Then, click the "Pay Now" button to begin a secure Paypal transaction in a new window.

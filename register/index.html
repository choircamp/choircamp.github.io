WELCOME
Welcome to AIC Choir Camp 2023 and thanks for your Campers and your Support! This year you may register as in the past, or you can take advantage of our new online registration. Registering online will make our work much easier. Either way, follow the instructions below. And thanks!!
HOW TO REGISTER

Three things must happen for your camper(s) to attend Choir Camp. Please read all three steps below before proceeding.

  1. Once per camper: Register using the Online Camper Registration Form.
    • This link will open the sectioned registration form in a new browser tab. Submit the full online form once per camper, then return to this page.
    • The online form might invite you to optionally sign in to save your progress. This is optional. It's a fairly short form.
    • If performing this registration step online is not convenient for you, print and fill out our Camper Registration Form once per camper and postal mail it to the address shown at the bottom of the printed form. And then complete Steps 2 and 3 below.
  2. Once for all campers: Pay the Registration Fee.
    • Camper Registration is finalized only upon full payment.
    • Submit a single payment for all campers, then return to this page.

    The cost of the registration is:

    First Camper Additional Campers (Same Family)
    $360.00 $330.00

    Provide information below, and then press the button. The payment processor will open in a new browser tab. Remember to return here when you're done.

    Camper Count
    Camper Last Name
    (so your payment is applied to the right registration)
    Camper First Name(s)
    (list several, if need be, separated by commas)
  3. Once per camper: Prepare your Medical Form. Print and fill out once per camper. Bring Medical Form and supporting documents with your camper(s) to camp! 😀
    • Per the Medical Form: COVID vaccine proof is again required for 2023 and will be verified before campers may remain at camp.
AFTER THE STEPS ABOVE

After you have registered and paid, your next task is to bring your printed Medical Form(s), accompanying medical documents, and happy camper(s) to camp!! We'll have you fill out a Release Form when you get here.

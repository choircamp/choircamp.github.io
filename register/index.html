OVERVIEW

Registering for AIC Choir Camp means doing all of three things:

  1. Tell Us You're Coming, online, via Google Forms (see below)
    A single form submission per camper.
  2. Pay Now or Later, online, via Paypal (see below)
    If you prefer, send a check instead, with your paperwork below.
  3. Send Paperwork, through the mail, via USPS (see below)
    Medical and Release forms (and a photo!)

Questions? Feedback? See the Contact Information on our homepage.

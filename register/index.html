HOW TO REGISTER

Registering your camper(s) for is as easy as the steps below. Reading all three steps before proceeding can help things go smoothly.

  1. Once per camper: Register using the Online Camper Registration Form.
    • This link will open the registration form in a new browser tab. Submit the full online form once per camper, then return to this page.
    • The online form might invite you to sign in to save your progress. This is optional. It's a fairly short form, shown in sections.
  2. Just once total - no matter how many campers: Pay the Registration Fee.
    • Submit a single payment for all campers, then return to this page.
    • Camper Registration is finalized only upon full payment.

    The full cost of registration is:

    First Camper Additional Campers (Same Family)
    $370.00 $340.00

    Below, you may pay the full registration or a deposit. If you have paid a deposit, return here to pay the balance later, or pay it when you drop off your child at camp.

    Provide information below, and then press the button. The payment processor will open in a new browser tab. Remember to return here when you're done.

    Camper Count
    Camper Last Name
    (so your payment is applied to the right registration)
    Camper First Name(s)
    (list several, if need be, separated by commas)
  3. Once per camper: Prepare your Medical Form. Print and fill out a separate form per camper. Bring Medical Form and supporting documents with your camper(s) to camp! 😀
AFTER THE STEPS ABOVE

After you have registered and paid, your next task is to bring the following to camp on Sunday, :

  • printed Medical Form(s)
  • any outstanding payment balance

We'll have you sign a Release Form when you arrive.

That's it! We'll see you and your happy campers at Subiaco! 😄 We can't wait!

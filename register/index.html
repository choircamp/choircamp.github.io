HOW TO REGISTER

Three things must happen for your camper(s) to attend Choir Camp this year. Please skim all three steps below before proceeding.

  1. Register your camper online using the Camper Registration Form.
    • This link will open the sectioned registration form in a new browser tab. Submit the full online form once per camper, then return to this page.
    • The form might invite you to optionally sign into your Google account to save your progress. This is optional. It's a fairly short form.

     

  2. Pay the Camper Registration Fee below.
    • The "Pay Now" button below will open the payment form in a new browser tab. Submit one combined payment for all campers, then return to this page.
    • Camper Registration is finalized only upon full payment.

     

  3. Prepare your Medical Form. Print and fill out the full form once per camper. Bring Medical Form and supporting documents with your camper(s) to camp! 😀
    • Per the Medical Form: COVID vaccine proof is required for 2023 and will be verified before campers may remain at camp.

When you have registered and paid for your camper(s), your next task is to bring your camper(s) and accompanying Medical Form(s) to camp.

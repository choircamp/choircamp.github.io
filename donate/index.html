WHAT

Choir Camp offers an intensive music program for children each summer, with classes in choral singing, handbells, liturgy, Broadway song & dance, history, poetry and art. This camp aims to provide a healthy balance between work and play.

Online Donation makes it easy to securely donate online to Choir Camp. We accept donations via Paypal.com, and you can donate even if you don't have a Paypal account.

We're sponsored by the Arkansas Interfaith Conference, a declared 501c3 not-for-profit organization. All donations are tax-deductible.

WHY

Your continued support helps to defray additional costs not covered by tuition. More than 10% of our campers receive scholarships funded by your generous contributions.

It is this loving spirit that inspires us each year. Help us continue this musical experience for Arkansas' children.

HOW

Simply click the "Donate" button below to open a secure Paypal transaction in a new window. You choose what to donate (no amount is too little). Donate as often as you like.

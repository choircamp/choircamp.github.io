BRING

Bring a salad or dessert (drinks will be furnished) and a donation for AIC Choir Camp. Contributions to Choir Camp make possible full or partial scholarships for many campers.

Founded by Trinity Cathedral in 1976, AIC Choir Camp has provided music and arts education to literally thousands of campers. The 2018 Choir Camp-the 43rd-begins on July 22 at Subiaco Abbey and Academy, culminating on Festival Day, Saturday, July 28.

WHAT

All participants -- EVER! -- of Choir Camp are invited to the Arkansas Choir Camp Reunion on Sunday, August 5th!

On Sunday, August 5th, Trinity Little Rock's Summer Sundays on Spring Street service will be sung by the Arkansas Interfaith Choir Camp campers and staff, current and previous.

If you have EVER attended Choir Camp -- started by Charlie Rigsby at Trinity many years ago -- you are invited and ENCOURAGED! to sing at this service. Please join us!

If you're coming from out of town and would like somewhere to stay, please email Victoria Harden -- members of Trinity Little Rock are hosting out-of-town travelers for the evening!

Dinner will be served afterward! It will be wonderful to see everyone! Sign up below!
